Getting started

  1. Open Ziggy and enter your name.
  2. On the Connect screen, tap Generate Code and share that code with your partner — or tap Join and enter their code.
  3. You'll both share one Ziggy. Feed, play, send photos and notes — everything syncs in real time. 💕

Common questions

My partner and I aren't synced. Make sure you both entered the same love code, and that both phones have an internet connection.

How do I disconnect? Settings → Disconnect. You can reconnect anytime with the same code.

How do I delete my data? Settings → Delete My Data permanently removes your shared pet, photos and messages.

Does it cost anything? Ziggy is free. No ads, no tracking.
